## ğŸ§  What Is _Probabilistic Robotics_?
Probabilistic robotics is a paradigm in robotics focused on handling uncertainty in robot perception and control. Instead of deterministic models, probabilistic methods explicitly model uncertainty and noise enabling robots to make robust decisions in real-world environments.

This approach includes topics such as:
- Bayesian state estimation and recursive filtering
- Kalman filters (standard, extended)
- Histogram and particle filters
- Localization and mapping (SLAM)
- Probabilistic models of perception and motion
